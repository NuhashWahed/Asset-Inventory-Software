IT Asset Inventory Software

IT Asset Inventory Software is a digital solution that helps organizations effectively track, manage, and organize their IT assets throughout their lifecycle. These assets include hardware (like computers, printers, routers), software licenses, peripheral devices, and more.

Key Features:

  • Asset Tracking: Maintain a centralized database of all IT assets with details such as serial numbers, locations, purchase/warranty dates, and assigned users.
  • Allocation & Deallocation: Easily assign assets to departments or employees, and deallocate them when no longer in use.
  • Damage & Maintenance Management: Mark assets as damaged, schedule maintenance, and get visual alerts for overdue services.
  • Branch & Department Organization: Categorize assets by branch or department for clearer visibility and accountability.
  • Audit & Compliance: Keep records ready for audits, and reduce the risk of compliance issues with proper license and warranty tracking.
